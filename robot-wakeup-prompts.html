START FRAME: A small 3D robot character in a sleeping/powered-down state. Head tilted down 35°, arms relaxed at sides, body slightly hunched. Eyes completely dark — no glow, no light. Robot floating very close (5px gap) above three concentric elliptical rings (gold #A68200, yellow #FFCF23, blue #146EEC) that glow dimly. Dark navy background #1E293B. Quiet, still, dim.

END FRAME: Same robot, now fully awake and waving enthusiastically. Head raised, right arm up in a friendly wave, eyes glowing bright electric blue (#146EEC) with visible white iris core. Robot has floated higher above the platform rings, which now glow brighter. Two glass-morphism Arabic text cards float on either side of the robot. Arabic headline "#FFCF23 حوّل شغفك إلى مهنة احترافية" visible at top. Scene is bright, warm, energetic.

ANIMATION SEQUENCE:

[0.0s – 0.4s] DORMANT HOLD
Complete stillness. Robot sleeping, eyes off, rings barely glowing. Establish the quiet resting state.

[0.4s – 1.0s] POWER-ON SEQUENCE
The three platform rings beneath the robot begin to pulse — a slow ripple emanates outward from the innermost ring (#146EEC) to the outer rings. Glow intensity increases from 15% → 60% over 0.6s. Simultaneously, the robot's eye sockets flicker once — a very brief flash (0.05s) of blue light then back to dark, like a system boot flicker.

[1.0s – 1.5s] EYES ILLUMINATE
The robot's eyes light up — not instantly, but with a smooth bloom effect: dark → dim blue → bright electric blue (#146EEC) over 0.5s. The iris pupils brighten to near-white in the center. A soft 30px radial glow emanates from each eye, casting faint blue light on the robot's face/chest. The robot's posture begins to straighten — head lifts ~10°.

[1.5s – 2.2s] BODY LIFT & WAKE
The robot's full body wakes with a smooth spring-eased upward motion:
- Head continues rising to full upright position
- Spine/torso straightens with a subtle bounce (overshoot 5° then settle)
- Arms begin to separate from body — left arm swings slightly out, right arm begins rising
- Robot floats upward ~40px from its starting position, moving away from the platform rings
- The rings below respond: all three rings brighten and expand slightly outward (5% scale increase), as if energized by the robot lifting off
Easing: spring physics — ease-in-out with a gentle overshoot and settle

[2.2s – 3.0s] WAVE GESTURE
The robot's right arm continues its arc upward — elbow bending, forearm rising, hand opening into a clear friendly wave. The wave motion has a slight secondary bounce: arm overshoots the final position by 10° then settles. The robot's body leans slightly in the direction of the wave (weight shift, feels natural). Head tilts slightly toward the waving arm with a friendly tilt of ~8°.

[3.0s – 3.6s] CARD ENTRANCE — CARD 1 (RIGHT SIDE)
The first glass-morphism card (dark navy background, gold border accent, Arabic text) slides in from the right side of frame. Motion: starts from 40px outside right edge, moves to final position with ease-out deceleration. Simultaneously fades from opacity 0 → 100%. As it arrives, a very subtle scale bounce (0.95 → 1.02 → 1.0) gives it a "snap into place" feel. Gold border accent (#FFCF23) glows briefly on arrival.

[3.6s – 4.2s] CARD ENTRANCE — CARD 2 (LEFT SIDE)
0.6s after card 1, the second card (blue border accent #146EEC) slides in from the left side — same animation mechanics, mirrored. The robot's head turns very slightly toward this card as it arrives, acknowledging it.

[4.2s – 5.0s] FINAL STATE HOLD
All elements settled: robot waving, eyes glowing, both cards visible, rings glowing steadily. A very slow, subtle float animation begins on the robot (sinusoidal hover: 6px up/down over 2s period). The rings have a slow continuous pulse glow. Hold this final state.

CAMERA: Completely locked throughout. Zero camera movement. No zoom, no pan, no rotation.

RING DETAILS: The three concentric rings maintain their colors throughout:
- Outer: #A68200 (dark gold)  
- Middle: #FFCF23 (bright yellow)
- Inner: #146EEC (electric blue)
Rings have a soft elliptical glow/shadow beneath the robot suggesting a reflective surface.

EASING: All motion uses spring physics or cubic ease-in-out. Nothing is linear. The wake-up feels organic — like a living creature, not a mechanical switch.

STYLE: Clean 3D animation. Same robot art style as reference images. Dark navy background (#1E293B) throughout. Color palette strictly: #FFCF23, #A68200, #146EEC, #1E293B.
