Prompt C · Video Model (Runway / Kling / Pika)
Cinematic Animation — 10–15s Seamless Loop
Upload Prompt A as start frame, Prompt B as end frame
START FRAME: Deep navy black screen. A single tiny luminous white dot (8px) appears at center frame with a soft radial glow — like a star igniting. Silence and stillness.

END FRAME: The Arabic word مسار fully formed in neon blue-to-purple gradient light, with four branching neon paths extending to frosted-glass labels (Frontend, Backend, AI, Mobile) across the frame. A larger network of faint nodes and paths glows softly in the background.

ANIMATION SEQUENCE — execute in exact order:

[0.0s – 0.8s] EMERGENCE: Black screen. A single luminous dot appears at center with a slow bloom — opacity 0 to 100% over 0.4s, then glow radius expands from 4px to 40px over 0.4s. Ease-in-out. Hold.

[0.8s – 4.5s] PATH DRAWING: The dot begins moving smoothly, leaving a glowing neon trail (2px line, blue-to-purple gradient). It traces the Arabic word مسار in correct right-to-left stroke order — following the natural calligraphic motion of each letterform. Speed: deliberate and confident, not rushed. Each completed stroke brightens slightly as if ink is setting. The total word takes ~3.5s to complete. Ease-in-out on direction changes.

[4.5s – 5.5s] WORD REVEAL: The fully-traced word pulses once — a gentle wave of brightness rolls through the letterforms from right to left. The outer glow expands and contracts once, like a heartbeat. The dot holds position at the word's end point.

[5.5s – 5.8s] BRANCH ANTICIPATION: The dot moves to the central branching junction of the word. A subtle circular ripple emits from it (like dropping a stone in water) — 3 concentric rings, neon blue, expanding outward, fading to 0 opacity. This signals the decision moment.

[5.8s – 8.0s] BRANCH GROWTH: Four neon paths grow simultaneously from the junction, extending outward toward their destinations (upper-left, upper-right, lower-left, lower-right). The paths draw themselves like living vines — bezier curves, smooth, eased. As each path reaches its endpoint, the frosted-glass label card fades in (opacity 0 to 100%, scale 0.8 to 1.0, ease-out). The AI branch path pulses with slightly higher brightness.

[8.0s – 8.6s] DOT DECISION: The dot pauses at junction. It emits one slow pulse of light. Then — it travels down the AI branch path, moving with confidence, leaving a brighter trail on that one branch.

[8.6s – 10.5s] PATH TRANSFORMATION: As the dot moves along the AI branch, the path behind it subtly transforms — abstract code characters (monospace, #4FC3F7, 20% opacity) float upward from the path like embers, and faint UI wireframe elements (rounded rectangles, progress bars, data nodes) materialize briefly beside the path, then dissolve. The camera very slowly and imperceptibly zooms out (0.95x to 1.0x scale, ease-in-out) to reveal more of the growing network.

[10.5s – 12.0s] NETWORK ZOOM: Camera continues slow zoom out. More distant nodes and branch paths become visible at low opacity (8–15%), suggesting a vast interconnected system growing from the word مسار.

[12.0s – 13.5s] LOOP FADE: All branches, labels, and network paths gracefully fade — opacity reduces from 100% to 0% over 1.5s, flowing inward toward the word مسار like energy returning to its source. The word itself dims last. Only the glowing dot remains, growing slightly brighter as everything else fades.

[13.5s – 14.0s] LOOP RESET: The word مسار fades to 0. The dot fades to 0. Screen is black for 0.3s. Then the dot re-emerges and the loop begins again seamlessly.

CAMERA: Completely locked — zero camera movement except the subtle slow zoom during network reveal (max 5% zoom over 4 seconds). No shake, no drift.

STYLE: Premium motion graphics. Linear, Vercel, Apple product video aesthetic. Every motion is eased — no linear or abrupt cuts. 
LIGHTING: All light is emitted from the neon elements themselves. Background stays pure dark navy throughout.
DURATION: 14 seconds, seamless loop.
ASPECT RATIO: 16:9.
FRAME RATE: 24fps minimum, 60fps preferred.
QUALITY: Maximum fidelity, no artifacts, no compression blur.
